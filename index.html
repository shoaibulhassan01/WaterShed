Key Features:

Eco-Awareness Staking

Stake Watershed coins to support water conservation projects and earn rewards.

Educative NFTs

Purchase and trade NFTs that spread knowledge about fresh water conservation, with proceeds going towards real-world clean water initiatives.

Proof-of-Hydro

Unique consensus mechanism where mining rewards are increased for areas actively engaged in water conservation.

Freshwater Marketplace

A decentralized platform where users can donate, invest, or collaborate on clean water projects using Watershed coins.

Eco-Friendly Mining

Ensures low energy consumption and minimal carbon footprint during the coin mining process.

Smart Contracts for Clean Water

Build and deploy smart contracts that facilitate agreements between communities, NGOs, and donors for water projects.
